Sustainable Postcard Materials

Postcards are a popular form of printed communication that can be made more environmentally friendly through the use of sustainable materials. Modern eco-friendly postcards maintain the same high quality while reducing environmental impact.

Key Features

  • 100% recycled paper options
  • FSC-certified materials
  • Biodegradable coatings
  • Durable for mailing
  • Compatible with eco-friendly inks

Environmental Impact

Traditional postcards often use virgin paper and harmful coatings. By switching to sustainable materials, we can significantly reduce the environmental footprint of postcard printing while maintaining professional quality. These eco-friendly alternatives use less water in production and create less waste.

Best Practices

For the most environmentally conscious postcard printing:

  • Choose recycled or FSC-certified paper
  • Use vegetable-based inks
  • Opt for minimal coating or eco-friendly alternatives
  • Print in standard sizes to minimize waste
