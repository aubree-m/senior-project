UV-cured inks are specially made inks that are cured in a UV light. This can be used to help the inks faster. This can give the design more vibrant colors. UV inks are about 99 percent of VOC free. This causes UV-cured inks to be more friendly to the environment. The concerns with UV-cured inks have a lot of emissions of VOC which can cause air pollution and health issues for humans and animals.
