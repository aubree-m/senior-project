What are Water Based Inks?

Water based inks are inks that use water to activate it. While water is the main carrier, they need other components such as plastics for proper adhesion and durability.

Key Benefits

  • Environmentally friendly
  • Low VOC emissions
  • Cost effective
  • Safe to use
  • Versatile applications

Considerations

Water based inks require a longer time to dry compared to other ink types. However, this drawback is offset by their environmental benefits and lower health risks.
