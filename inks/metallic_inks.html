What are Metallic Inks?

Metallic ink is a special type of printing ink with small metallic particles.

Key Benefits

- makes the design look high end - makes it look more expensive - makes the color better - cost effective

Applications

- easy to clean - can be used for diffrent thickness - can be blended with diffrent inks - better for larger area of color
