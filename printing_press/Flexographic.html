What is Flexographic Printing?

Flexographic printing is a modern form of rotary relief printing that uses flexible photopolymer printing plates wrapped around rotating cylinders. This method is particularly effective for continuous patterns and is widely used in packaging and label printing.

The Flexographic Process

  • Creation of flexible photopolymer plates
  • Mounting plates on printing cylinders
  • Application of fast-drying inks
  • High-speed rotary printing process
  • Quick drying and finishing

Environmental Considerations

  • Water-based and solvent-free ink options
  • Recyclable printing plates
  • Reduced waste through precise ink control
  • Energy-efficient drying systems
  • Sustainable substrate options

Sustainability Impact

Modern flexographic printing has evolved to become more environmentally friendly. The industry has moved towards water-based inks, reducing VOC emissions. Additionally, improvements in plate technology and ink delivery systems have significantly reduced waste and improved efficiency. The ability to use recycled materials and eco-friendly substrates makes flexographic printing a viable option for sustainable packaging solutions.
